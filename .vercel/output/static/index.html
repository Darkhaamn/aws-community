Our Purpose

Building Mongolia's Cloud Future

The Mongolian AWS Community is dedicated to fostering cloud computing knowledge and skills across Mongolia. We bring together students, professionals, and businesses to learn, share experiences, and grow together in the AWS ecosystem.

  • Knowledge Sharing

    Regular workshops, webinars, and hands-on sessions to share AWS knowledge and best practices.

  • Career Growth

    Certification preparation, mentorship, and networking opportunities to advance your cloud career.

  • Innovation

    Collaborative projects and hackathons to solve local challenges using AWS technologies.
